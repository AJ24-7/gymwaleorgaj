Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform and services.

What is Gym-Wale?

Gym-Wale is India's leading gym discovery platform that helps you find the perfect gym based on your location, budget, and fitness preferences. We offer flexible membership options including unique per-day plans.

How do per-day plans work?

Our innovative per-day plans allow you to pay only for the days you actually visit the gym. Perfect for people with irregular schedules or those who want to try different gyms without long-term commitments.

Do you offer personal training services?

Yes! We connect you with certified personal trainers available at partner gyms. You can book personalized training sessions based on your fitness goals and schedule.

What membership plans are available?

We offer various membership tiers including Basic, Premium, and our unique Per-Day plans. Each gym may have different pricing structures and benefits to suit different needs and budgets.

How do I find gyms near me?

Simply use our search feature above, enter your city or pincode, or click "Use My Location" to automatically find nearby gyms. You can filter by activities, price range, and other preferences.

Are the gym reviews authentic?

Absolutely! All reviews are from verified gym members who have actually visited the facilities. We have strict policies against fake reviews to maintain authenticity and help you make informed decisions.
